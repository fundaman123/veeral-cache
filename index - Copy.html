What We Do

Veeral Controls is a global power-electronics engineering company delivering mission-grade, high-density conversion systems for precision high-current and high-voltage applications.

With 30+ years of deep domain expertise, we design and build ultra-fast converters, multi-MW inverter platforms, high-current rectifiers, precision magnet supplies, and advanced IGBT/SiC power stacks deployed across renewable energy, research accelerators, rail traction, defence, and critical infrastructure.

Our integrated engineering—spanning wide-bandgap semiconductors, multi-pulse rectification, advanced magnetics, real-time control, and thermal-electromagnetic simulation—enables robust, modular systems optimised for dynamic response, harmonic performance, and long operational life.

Trusted worldwide by OEMs and mission-critical industries, Veeral delivers proven power-conversion platforms built to the highest global standards.
