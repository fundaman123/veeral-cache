Ruggedised Power Electronics & Controllers

Veeral designs mission-grade power-electronic systems engineered to operate in extreme defence environments. These ruggedised controllers and converter modules are built to withstand vibration, shock, temperature extremes, electromagnetic stress, and continuous high-load operation.

Highlights

  • Defence-grade design, thermal robustness & long operational life
  • High-reliability power modules for propulsion, actuation & weapons subsystems
  • EMI/EMC-hardened architecture compliant with stringent defence standards
  • Real-time digital control platforms built for fast dynamic response
  • Proven performance in field deployments and harsh duty cycles

Applications

Armoured vehicles, naval systems, missile support electronics, radar subsystems, mobile power units.
