Specialised High-Voltage & Precision Modules

These high-voltage and ultra-precision power modules are engineered for defence systems requiring exceptional accuracy, fast rise times, and stable operation under heavy electrical and environmental stresses. Veeral’s HV modules include multi-kV generators, precision magnet supplies, and fast-switching power blocks used in advanced defence technologies.

Highlights

  • High-voltage generator modules based on ZVS, multi-level & wide-bandgap technologies
  • Precision power supplies with ultra-low ripple (up to 100 ppm)
  • Fast switching performance for pulsed-power and high-frequency applications
  • Modular, compact designs tailored for mission-specific requirements
  • Built to meet long-duration, high-stability operation requirements

Applications

Defence radars, directed-energy systems, electromagnetic launch systems, precision actuation, pulsed power units.
